
               ********* PLEASE NOTE *********

Before installing any of the software products on this CD, you
should read the product's Installation Guide and the release
notes.  These documents contain valuable information about 
configuring your system and installing the product.

As a reminder, you must register a Product Authorization Key (PAK)
before the installation in order to use the product on the CD.

You can use menu selection 5 of this utility to move the product's
release notes into the SYS$HELP directory, where they can be 
displayed or printed.  If you need further assistance, you may
use menu selection 1 to invoke HELP, and enter 6 to receive 
assistance.
